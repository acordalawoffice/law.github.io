        <h3 style="font-size: 2.0rem; color: #1a202c; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 10px;">13th-Month Pay: What Counts and What Doesn't in Your Year-End Computations</h3>
        <div class="article-meta" style="font-size: 0.85rem; color: #718096; margin-bottom: 20px;">Published: July 2026 | Category: Labor Standards</div>

        <div class="article-body" style="color: #2d3748; line-height: 1.7; text-align: justify;">
            <p style="margin-bottom: 20px;">
                Presidential Decree No. 851 mandates that all rank-and-file employees who have worked for at least one (1) month within a calendar year are entitled to <strong>13th-month pay</strong>. This must be paid on or before <strong>December 24th</strong> annually.
            </p>

            <h4 style="font-size: 1.15rem; color: #2d3748; margin-top: 25px; margin-bottom: 12px;">The Core Computation Formula:</h4>
            <div style="background: #f8fafc; padding: 18px; border-radius: 6px; text-align: center; font-size: 1.05rem; color: #1a202c; margin: 20px 0; font-family: 'Courier New', Courier, monospace; border: 1px solid #e2e8f0; font-weight: bold; overflow-x: auto; white-space: nowrap;">
                13th Month Pay = (Total Basic Salary Earned Inside the Calendar Year) / 12
            </div>

            <h4 style="font-size: 1.15rem; color: #2d3748; margin-top: 25px; margin-bottom: 12px;">What is Excluded from "Basic Salary"?</h4>
            <p style="margin-bottom: 15px;">Unless specified otherwise by your company policy or an existing Collective Bargaining Agreement (CBA), the basic salary does not include:</p>
            <ul style="padding-left: 24px; margin-bottom: 20px;">
                <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Cost of Living Allowances (COLA)</li>
                <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Unused vacation or sick leave cash conversions</li>
                <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Overtime pay, night shift differentials, and holiday premiums</li>
                <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Discretionary or performance-based profit bonuses</li>
            </ul>

            <blockquote style="margin: 25px 0; padding: 15px 20px; background: #fdfaf2; border-left: 4px solid #D4AF37; font-style: italic; color: #444;">
                <strong>Note on Absences:</strong> Maternity leave periods and unpaid absences reduce the "Total Basic Salary Earned" numerator, naturally adjusting the final prorated calculation downward.
            </blockquote>
