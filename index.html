Counsel Profile

Atty. Rodel P. Acorda

Managing Counsel / Labor Law Practitioner

Atty. Rodel P. Acorda has been an active legal practitioner in the Philippines for nearly three decades, focusing on Philippine labor and employment law, corporate compliance, and workplace governance. Following his admission to the Philippine Bar in 1998, he served for over four years as an Associate Attorney at a law firm in Makati City. During this tenure, his practice involved handling seafarer claims, maritime disputes, and civil, criminal, and labor litigation. This background provided the foundational experience for his subsequent transition into independent practice.

Now based in Quezon City, Atty. Acorda advises corporate clients and executives on employment frameworks and regulatory compliance. His practice includes assisting businesses with corporate restructuring, drafting workplace policies, and managing employer risk under the Labor Code. Having represented both corporate employers and individual complainants in separate matters throughout his career, his advisory approach incorporates insights from both employee-side claims and management-side defense.

Atty. Acorda's practice includes litigation before the National Labor Relations Commission (NLRC), where he represents clients through the Single Entry Approach (SEnA conciliation-mediation) and formal arbitration tracks. He also handles regulatory and compliance matters before the Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE) and the Department of Migrant Workers (DMW), including the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration (POEA) and the Overseas Workers Welfare Administration (OWWA), for both local and international employment concerns.

Practice Focus

Workplace Governance & Policy Design

  • Drafting, structuring, and aligning comprehensive Employee Handbooks and operational frameworks with active DOLE standards.
  • Formulating legally resilient executive and general employment agreements (Probationary, Project, Seasonal, Fixed-Term, and Regular Status).
  • Auditing corporate architectures and providing preventive legal updates to isolate and eradicate operational liability risks.

Regulatory Advisory & Operational Compliance

  • Structuring internal disciplinary workflows under the statutory Two-Notice Rule framework to insulate management actions.
  • Calibrating Notice to Explain (NTE) blueprints, corporate administrative protocols, and legal frameworks for downscaling or valid redundancy actions.
  • Strategic advice and enterprise representation during mandatory Single-Entry Approach (SEnA) conciliation-mediation conferences.

Complex Labor Dispute & Employment Litigation

  • Defending complex corporate employer entities against illegal dismissal actions, unfair labor practices, and heavy monetary claims before Labor Arbiters.
  • Enforcing and litigating POEA Standard Employment Contract (POEA-SEC) parameters, sea/land-based OFW claims, and agency joint liability defenses.
  • Drafting definitive, high-stakes Position Papers, Pleadings, and Appeals before the NLRC, Court of Appeals, and Supreme Court under CPRA standards.
